## Responsible AI

Responsible AI considers the benefits and potential harms that AI systems can bring to society and is based on six core principle goals:
- Accountability goals 
- Transparency goals
- Fairness goals
- Reliability & safety goals
- Privacy & Security goals
- Inclusiveness goals



Accountability goals
- Impact assessment
- Oversight of significant adverse impacts
- Fit for purpose 
- Data governance and management
- Human oversight and control

Transparency goals
- System intelligibility for decision making
- Communication to stakeholders 
- Disclosure of AI interaction

Fairness goals
- Quality of service
- Allocation of resources and opportunities
- Minimization of stereotyping, demeaning, and erasing outputs

Reliability & safety goals
- Reliability and safety guidance
- Failures and remediations
- Ongoing monitoring, feedback, and evaluation

Privacy & Security goals
- Privacy Standard compliance
- Security Policy compliance

Inclusiveness goals
- Accessibility Standards compliance

Answers
As we can see, the Responsible AI Standard is a set of company-wide principles that aim to ensure, enable, and explain the best model for developing and deploying AI.
